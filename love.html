My dearest Sai,

You turn my ordinary days into something I look forward to. The way you laugh, the way you talk about the things you love, the way you just exist it all makes my world feel calmer, brighter, and a lot more beautiful, even from afar.

I love the little things: the sound of your voice, the comfort I feel just seeing your name pop up on my phone, and the way you make me feel close to you even when we’re apart. I could spend hours just listening to you, wishing I was holding you, or just being on a call in silence with you, and it would still never feel like enough time.

Thank you for choosing to be my Valentine. I promise to keep choosing you, in each message, in each late-night call, in every thought of you, and until the day I can finally kiss you for real.

With all of my love,
Toto Dave
